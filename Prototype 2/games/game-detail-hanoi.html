    Tower of Hanoi

    Moves: 0

    Move all the disks from the left peg to the right peg, keeping them in the same order (largest on bottom, smallest on top).

    Only one disk can be moved at a time.

    You can only move the top disk of a peg.

    You cannot place a larger disk on top of a smaller disk.

    Minimum moves to solve is 2^n - 1, n being the number of disks
